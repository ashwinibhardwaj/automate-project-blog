Stock Trend Prediction with LSTM

Posted on: June 24, 2025

Why I Built This Project

The stock market is an ever-evolving landscape, shaped by trends, patterns, and unpredictable shifts. Understanding these movements can offer valuable insights, but traditional analysis often feels overwhelming and complex for beginners.

With this in mind, I developed a Stock Trend Prediction system powered by LSTM, a model that excels at learning from sequential data. The goal was to make stock forecasting more accessible and interactive, allowing users to simply input a stock ticker and instantly visualize historical trends and future predictions using an intuitive Streamlit dashboard.

The Problem I Wanted to Solve

Stock forecasting can be incredibly complex. Traditional methods require deep domain knowledge, and many beginners struggle to get started. I wanted to simplify this by building an accessible tool that:

  • Allows users to input stock tickers directly.
  • Displays moving averages to visualize trends.
  • Predicts future stock prices using LSTM, a time-series-friendly model.
  • Makes the process fully interactive and easy to use, even for non-coders.

The goal was to provide a fast, interactive, and beginner-friendly stock analysis experience.

My Solution: Stock Trend Predictor

I built a web-based stock prediction tool using Streamlit, where users can type a stock ticker, visualize past trends, and explore future predictions generated by an LSTM model.

  • Stock Input: Users can simply enter the stock ticker symbol they want to analyze.
  • Historical Visualization: The system shows moving averages (100 days, 200 days) to reveal trends clearly.
  • Predictive Graphs: LSTM-powered predictions are plotted on interactive graphs for easy understanding.
  • Streamlit Interface: Everything is integrated into an intuitive, real-time web application.

How It Works

  1. Step 1: Enter a stock ticker (e.g., AAPL, TSLA) into the application.
  2. Step 2: View moving averages and past price trends instantly.
  3. Step 3: Get future stock trend predictions using LSTM‚Äôs sequence-based learning.
  4. Step 4: Interact with the generated graphs and analyze potential future movements.

Challenges I Faced

  • Understanding how to properly prepare and scale time-series data for LSTM models.
  • Balancing between overfitting and underfitting in sequential predictions.
  • Creating responsive Streamlit charts that display moving averages alongside predictions.
  • Ensuring the predictions stay intuitive and visually clear for the end user.

What I Learned

  • Deepened my understanding of LSTM and its sequence learning capabilities.
  • Enhanced my skills in building interactive dashboards with Streamlit.
  • Gained experience working with real stock market datasets and time-series analysis.
  • Improved my ability to make machine learning tools accessible and user-friendly.
